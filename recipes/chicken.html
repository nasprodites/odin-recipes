Grilled Chicken Recipe

If you follow the steps below, your dinner will look like this picture!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breast
  • Stubs marinade

Steps

  1. Add chicken and Stubs marinade to a one gallon ziplock bag.
  2. Let chicken marinate for 12 - 24 hours.
  3. Grill chicken for about six minutes on each side. Baste generously.
  4. Let chicken cook to about 160 degrees.
