Cooked Rice

If you follow the steps below, your rice will look like this picture!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice.
  • Salt.
  • Butter.

Steps

  1. Add salt and butter to water.
  2. Bring water to a boil.
  3. Add two cups of rice to boiling water.
  4. Reduce heat to simmer. Cook for 12 minutes.
